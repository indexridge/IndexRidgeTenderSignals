# Legal and Copyright Risk Policy

This is an operational risk-control policy, not legal advice.

## Residual risk

No public business is literally zero-liability. The goal is to keep risk low by using official/open public data, clear attribution, conservative wording, no copied third-party creative material, no personal-data enrichment, no regulated advice, and no guarantees.

## Allowed by default

- Official/public procurement notice fields from sources with explicit reuse rights.
- Open Government Licence data with required attribution.
- Factual summaries and category tags generated from notice fields.
- Links back to official notice pages.
- Downloadable CSV/JSON/ZIP packs generated from approved open sources.

## Not allowed without explicit review

- Copying tender attachments, PDFs, images, logos, maps, news articles, paid-platform data, screenshots, or proprietary commentary.
- Claiming official status, complete coverage, guaranteed lead quality, guaranteed revenue, bid success, compliance, or suitability for due diligence.
- Providing legal, procurement, bid-writing, financial, investment, tax, planning, engineering, or regulatory advice.
- Emailing prospects, adding analytics/signups, running ads, or taking payment before the user approves the exact account/platform/copy.
- Enriching tender records with personal contact details or building personal outreach lists.

## Mandatory public disclaimer

> IndexRidge Tender Signals and IndexRidge Public Data Packs are informational public-data monitoring products. They are not legal, procurement, bid-writing, financial, investment, tax, compliance, planning, engineering, environmental, or regulatory advice. Records may be incomplete, delayed, duplicated, incorrectly classified, or missing context. Users must verify all information against the relevant official source before acting.
